The Vision: A New Era for Campus

This project transcends a simple renovation. It's a strategic transformation to create a vibrant, multi-use destination that serves as the operational heart of Pallacanestro Varese and a valuable asset for the entire community.

Corporate Headquarters

Centralizing operations for Pallacanestro Varese and anchor tenant Luis Scola Group to drive synergy.

Elite Training Facilities

Commercial & Wellness Hub

Dedicated spaces for retail, restaurant, and wellness services with confirmed tenant interest.

Sustainable Revenue

Creating a self-sustaining financial asset with diversified income streams.
